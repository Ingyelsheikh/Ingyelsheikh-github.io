About Me

I am a univeristy student, so inherintley I am always stressed. I find myself always revising lists of things I have to do in my head. My mind was never quiet. This is until I decided to take half an hour daily to just breath and relax with my cup of coffee.

Purpose

I created this website as a safe haven for anyone who feels the same way I did. This page will serve as a reminder for anyone who is being too hard on themselves. Visiit this page for some self care advise, relaxtion tips, and for just taking inspiration to create your own healthy lifestyle

Who Is This Website For?

Everyone! This website is for anyone and everyone. Everyone deserves to cherish the small things in life that make it amazing! Everyone deserves to relax and take care of themselves. Whenever you feel like you're under the dumps , come here , and have a cup of coffee!
